Omni-Shell: The Last Epoch

Welcome to the Matrioshka Brain—a massive, failing Dyson sphere surrounding a star. You are a post-human consciousness living within this machine. Reality is a computational substrate where ships are mobile processors, and combat is both physical and informational.

1. The Core Objective: Processing is Power

In Omni-Shell, the ultimate goal isn't just to "destroy a base," but to secure the resources required for your digital existence.

  • Cycle-Time (CT): This is the universal currency representing literal seconds of processing power. You need it to exist, upgrade, and repair.
  • Control the Cores: Ships and stations run on Processor Cores. Disabling these is the primary way to win a battle, as it turns enemy vessels into salvageable derelicts.
  • Factions: Different groups compete to either rebuild the machine, preserve its current state, or seek transcendence elsewhere.

2. The Dual-Layer Battlefield

Every battle happens in two dimensions simultaneously. You can switch between them, or coordinate with teammates who specialize in one layer.

Layer Who Plays Here? Your Goal
Physical Layer Pilots, Gunners, Engineers Focus on kinetic combat. Use lasers and railguns to blast through the ship's Voxels (blocks) to create entry points.
Ghost Layer Hackers, Wraiths, Architects Phase inside enemy ships as a "Ghost." Sabotage internal systems like shields or life support while physical combat rages outside.

3. Economy & Currencies

  • Cycle-Time (CT): Earned through missions, selling resources, or even renting out your own processing power to others.
  • Sub-Routines: These are AI crew members. They have personalities and skills. You must keep them loyal and "patch" their code to keep them effective.
  • Voxel Construction: Ships are built block-by-block. Every block (thrusters, shields, processors) has a function and a CT cost to build.

4. Convoy Logistics (The Strategy Loop)

Resources aren't teleported; they must be physically moved between the Shells (layers) of the star-machine.

  • The Trade: You haul raw materials from the inner layers to the outer layers to make a profit.
  • The Risk: Moving a convoy makes you a target. You must balance the cost of hiring escorts (players or Sub-Routines) against the potential profit of the trip.
  • The Reward: Large-scale logistics allow you to fund massive fleet operations and high-end digital upgrades.

5. Basic Terminology

  • Architecture: Your "Class" (e.g., Monolith for speed/precision, Archive for storage/tanking).
  • Tether: The digital line connecting your Ghost form to your body. If an enemy hacker cuts this, you are trapped or "killed."
  • ICE: "Intrusion Countermeasure Electronics"—automated AI guards that hunt hackers in the Ghost Layer.
  • Breach: A hole in the ship's physical hull that allows Ghosts to enter.
